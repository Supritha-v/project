HOW TO REACH

Chickmagaluru is well connected by road and regular bus and cab services are available to and from banglore. the nearest airport is manglore airport. chickmagaluru railway line is under construction and should be functional sometime in 2013. hence, the nearest railway station is kadur(40km) and can be used by tourist to access this city.
