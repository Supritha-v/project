ABOUT CHICKMAGALURU

chickmagaluru is a beautifull district situated on the western part of karnatake. it is mostly popular for its coffee plantations and a pleasent climate.Another renoved attraction that chickmagaluru is known for its bababudhangiri ranges. though it was among the underexplored destinations, the hill station thanks to its picturesque landscape and greenery has earned a lot of attention from tourist across. the literal meaning of chickmagaluru is " Land of the Younger Daughter". chickmagaluru is famous for its serene environment, lust green forests and tall mountains. it is also famous for the coffee and is often reffered to as the "COFFEE LAND OF KARNATAKA". karnataks's coffe industry is in chickmagalruru and that makes it even more special. the coffe seed was planted here for the first time in india in the baba budan giri hills. coffe seeds were brought into the region by baba budan, a muslim saint, afer his visit to arabia in 1670 AD.the beans that were planted by him on the slope of chickmagaluru flourished, and the famous indian coffe industry was born. A walk among coffe plantation, especially during the flowing season(march-april) is an experiance that every tourist must do to enjoy the fresh air that is full of the coffe fragrance.
